Earlier version compiled for Pitzer College, Claremont, Ca. Link to Pitzer

* This site contains links to and original material on the history and culture of women of color in the U.S. and other countries. It includes articles, bibliographies, biographical information and resources.
* The term "women of color" here means women of African, American Indian/Native American, Asian, Latina/Hispanic and Middle Eastern descent, and includes multiethnic women.
* This site also contains bibliographies compiled by students who were enrolled in the Women of Color class at Pitzer College in the Spring of 1996.
